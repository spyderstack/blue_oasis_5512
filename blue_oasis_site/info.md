Neighborhood highlights
This home is located in the quiet, residential Bayshore Gardens area of Bradenton. The neighborhood has a relaxed Florida feel, with peaceful streets and easy access to local shops, parks, and dining. You're a short drive from downtown Bradenton, the Riverwalk, and the beautiful Gulf beaches that keep the area so popular with visitors. It’s a convenient spot for families and groups who want both tranquility and quick access to Sarasota and Bradenton attractions.
Getting around
Car Recommended:
Most guests choose to drive for the easiest access to beaches, shops, and restaurants. Major roads are nearby, making it simple to get around Bradenton and Sarasota.

Public Transit:
Manatee County Area Transit (MCAT) operates bus routes throughout the region, helpful for basic local travel.

Rideshare:
Uber and Lyft are widely available for quick trips without needing a car.

Walking & Biking:
The immediate area is walkable for short strolls, though most attractions are best reached by car. Riverwalk and downtown Bradenton offer great spots for walking or biking once you're there.
